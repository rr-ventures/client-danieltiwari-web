About

Hey, I'm Daniel.

My work revolves around transforming the unique potential of the ambitious individual into an enjoyable, fulfilling, and meaningful life, despite the struggles that naturally go along with being human.

Thinker · Life Coach · Mentor Read on
01 — How I got here

It took me almost a decade.

I know through personal experience how exhausting and frustrating it is to be dissatisfied yet feel stuck in life — often for reasons you aren't even fully aware of.

Until I trained as a coach myself, I had never worked with one. It took me almost a decade to gain the insights I needed to slowly, steadily, climb out of the deep rut I was in. The insights ranged from finding appealing answers to fundamental questions like what is the purpose of life?, all the way up to figuring out what I actually wanted and how to get there in today's complex world. Uncovering the limiting beliefs and inner conflicts that were holding me back — and quietly causing a lot of suffering — was a big part of it.

As nihilistic, disheartened, and lost as I had once been, I can assure you that that does not have to be the final destination.

My own journey turned into my vocation: coaching and mentoring dissatisfied yet ambitious individuals who are struggling, for whatever reasons, to make use of their potential or to live a truly satisfying life. I am so fascinated by the unique potential each of us brings into the world that it genuinely breaks my heart to imagine someone spending years, decades, or even a lifetime settling for less than the life they would truly find worth living — simply because they didn't have access to someone who believed in them and could help them tap into it.

"When a bubbling spring flows out from the mountains, it's doing its thing. And if a thirsty traveller helps himself, well that's fine. And so I talk in the same spirit."

Alan Watts

Accepting · Changing · Knowing the difference.

Simple, right? But as you may know, this is often far easier said than done. I spent an unnecessary number of years dissatisfied and frustrated with my life because I was too stubborn and proud to make use of tools such as coaching. I ended up having to invest so much more time, energy, and suffering than I would have, had I simply gotten someone like myself on board.

02 — What I want for you

What we're aiming for.

The six things that, in my experience, make a life worth living.

01

An attractive vision.

Every day existing within a context of life that fuels you with inspiration and positive energy.

02

Freedom & individuality.

The internal freedom and confidence to live authentically — rather than constantly making yourself fit life.

03

Your version of success.

By removing limitations, your performance becomes equivalent to your inherent potential. Success follows almost inevitably.

04

Joy & meaningfulness.

A life full of activities you genuinely enjoy, or that are at least fulfilling and meaningful to you.

05

Personal growth.

Living in a way that enables you to grow as the individual you truly are, rather than becoming a better actor.

06

Making hardship worth it.

Let's face the facts. There are times in life that may not be great — but we can at least make them worth it.

03 — What working together looks like

When we work together,
you will have the opportunity to:

i

Express yourself openly & honestly in a non-judgemental, confidential safe space.

ii

Gain clarity and understanding over the situation you're actually in.

iii

Receive honest, constructive criticism — including on the blind spots you cannot yet see yourself.

iv

Uncover, debunk, and replace false and limiting beliefs.

v

Determine who you actually are behind the social masks and false ideals.

vi

Discover that vision for your life that fuels you with energy.

vii

Gain an accurate model of how life and the world out there actually work.

viii

Create authentic strategies to implement change that actually works for you.

ix

Check in regularly to deal with whatever comes up along the way.
