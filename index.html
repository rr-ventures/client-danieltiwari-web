Questions

Who do you actually work with?

Ambitious people — often building something of their own — who look successful from the outside but feel they are living a version of themselves they were handed. If life already looks fine on paper and still feels off, that is exactly the gap I work in.

Is this therapy?

No. This is coaching and mentoring focused on identity, direction, and the patterns keeping you where you are. It is reflective and demanding rather than clinical. If what you need is therapy, I will tell you plainly.

What does it cost, and how long is it?

The work is a six-month, one-to-one engagement — I deliberately keep the numbers off the website. Fit comes first. If the diagnostic conversation shows we are a match, I will walk you through exactly what it involves and what it costs, with no pressure either way.

Do we meet in person?

We work online, so it does not matter where you are. I coach in English and German.

What is the assessment, and is it really free?

Yes, it is free and private. It is a short self-assessment that maps where life is asking for more honesty — the area costing you the most, the pattern keeping it in place, and how ready you actually are to change it. No pitch attached.
