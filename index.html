01 — The premise

A life worth living.

We all have a vision of a life we would love to live, even if we aren't yet aware of what that life would look like. Considering the extremely limiting culture we grow up in, it's no surprise that many of us wake up to an unfulfilling life, including the unpleasant feelings that go along with that.

The way I have come to see things, however, is that the sole purpose of existence is the enjoyment of it, and that we're all sitting on unrecognised potential to truly do so.

No matter what your current circumstances may be, the goal of my work is to enable you to not only freely thrive in life but to also embrace every waking moment of it in a way that may still be unimaginable to you.

"Until you make the unconscious conscious, it will direct your life and you will call it fate."

Carl Jung

Performance = Potential − Limitations

The degree to which we are able to thrive in life is the degree to which we are not limiting ourselves. You already have all of the inherent potential you need to achieve the life you would truly find worth living.

In today's world of opportunity, successful individuals aren't successful because their potential is higher than everyone else's, but because their performance is not being compromised by self-imposed limitations.

02 — The work

Three areas of focus.

Where the freedom you're looking for is actually buried.

01 / Attitude

The point of departure.

We pave the way to consciously and effectively co-create the life and experiences we want, instead of remaining passive victims of circumstance. Characterised by responsibility, authority, commitment, freedom, humility, and self-honesty.

02 / Metaphysics

The foundation underneath.

Our beliefs give rise to the meaning our circumstances carry, which gives rise to how we feel and act. The fundamental beliefs our culture holds around existence aren't only questionable; they create fear, suffering, and severe limitation. Without debunking them, real freedom stays out of reach.

03 / The Human Game

Playing to win.

Instead of thinking about life, we live it. We reach this point when we are no longer blocked emotionally or intellectually. Once we have realised being human is the only game in town, we give up seeking and become fully available to the experiences this life has to offer. This area is about achieving the life circumstances you would actually love.
