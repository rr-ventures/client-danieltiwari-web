The work

For extraordinary individuals who want to live extraordinary lives.

I offer long-term coaching relationships, one to one, for people committed to living life on their own terms — and unwilling to settle for less. I am a thinker before I am a coach: the work is direct, private, and honest.

The premise

Who this is for.

This is for you if

  • You honour authenticity, trust, and integrity.
  • You're committed to living life on your own terms, and won't settle for less.
  • You accept full authority, ownership, and responsibility over your life.
  • You prefer truth over comforting lies.
  • You value yourself, your life, and your time — and know you are your greatest asset.

I'm probably not for you if

  • You're looking for someone to coddle, motivate, or validate you.
  • You want a superficial, short-term "quick fix".
  • You give up when things get uncomfortable.
  • You take constructive criticism personally.
  • You're unwilling to be honest with yourself, or with me.
